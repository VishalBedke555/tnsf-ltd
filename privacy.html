1. Information We Collect

TNSF LTD (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. When you visit our website, request a quote, or contact us via forms, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, project details, and any other information you voluntarily provide. We also collect non-personal data like browser type, IP address, and pages visited to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to inquiries, provide construction estimates, communicate project updates, improve our website experience, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively for TNSF LTD business operations and client servicing.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your data with trusted subcontractors or partners only when necessary to deliver a construction service you requested (e.g., architectural consultants, material suppliers). These parties are bound by confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and restricted access to personal data. While no internet transmission is 100% secure, we continuously update our safeguards. Your contact details and project information are stored in secure systems with limited employee access.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and remember preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some interactive features of the site. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including California Consumer Privacy Act – CCPA and GDPR protections), you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of non-essential communications. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.
