Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Founded by Thumuganti Vineeth, TNSF LTD started with a simple mission: bring integrity, innovation, and superior craftsmanship to every construction project across the United Kingdom. What began as a small contracting team has grown into a trusted name in residential and commercial building.

With headquarters in Leicester, UK, and operations serving UK clients, we blend international standards with local building codes. Our team takes pride in transparent communication, safety excellence, and delivering projects that stand the test of time.
